ABOUT

Sync is an intra-college symposium hosted by the Department of Computer Science. It is conducted with the notion of aggrandizing the intellect and kindling the passion for computer science in budding engineers. Sync promotes inter-department collaboration and interdisciplinary activities. Apart from technical events, it focuses on other nontechnical events to maintain the perfect equilibrium between tech and non-tech events.

Sync ‘22 is about to blossom afresh on 28th and 29th of November, 2022. Encompassing multifarious events, this version has the benchmark features to enthrall the multitude. Every experience brings a greater clarity of vision and thus with the past experiences, Sync ‘22 is all set to reach further horizons.

Events

TECH EVENTS


OSPC

It’s a fast-paced programming contest, where the innovative minds come together to resolve challenges in the most complicated coding questions, involving data structures and algorithms.


Hello World!

The event is aimed at introducing the newbies to logical reasoning, arithmetic and programming concepts.The questions in this event will be assessing their critical thinking rather than their hardcore programming knowledge.


Code For Queens

It’s a women only programming event where the CodHers will have to put forth their ingenious solutions to win the crown.


Dataverse

The participant will be given a dataset to work with and they will have to train and build a model using the given dataset. They can make use of any pre-existing libraries and techniques to produce the best results.


Bugfender

This event tests the debugging skills of the participants. Given a program overflowing with bugs and logical errors, the participant will have to resolve the issues, make the program run as expected and produce the right results.


D - Code

This is a cryptography based round, which will be a story-based question to solve puzzles which will test the cryptographic knowledge of the candidates.

NON-TECH EVENTS


Knives Out

A murder mystery event where the participant will have to find who the murderer is using the clues and also present the logic and their train of thought that made them to zero on the killer.


Are You Not Entertained?

This is an entertainment Trivia Quiz catering to anyone looking to test their knowledge in Cinema, Sports and what not!


Snap Hunt

The participants will be given photos of some not so frequently visited places in our college. The participants will have to go to the place in the photo, take a selfie and send it to the organizers. Fastest teams will take home the prize.


Sadhuranga Vettai

This is a blitz tournament where each player gets 3 minutes and 2 seconds increment for each player. There will be a total of 5 rounds.


Meme Master

The participant will be given a theme or a template and they will have to use their creativity to make a meme out of it.
